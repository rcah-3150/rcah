Overview

Chartered on 23 February 2021 under Rotary International (District 3150), the Rotary Club of Abhyuday Hyderabad is a dynamic platform of service, fellowship, and community impact. Guided by its motto—“Powered by Purpose & Humanity: Community, Health & Social Impact Together for a Better Tomorrow”—the club blends youthful energy with Rotary’s timeless values to create lasting change.

What We Do

  • Environment: Organized impactful lake cleanups at Ameenpur Lake and with Wipro, mobilizing 120+ volunteers and collecting nearly 250 kg of waste, reinforcing our commitment to a cleaner, greener world.
  • Youth & Education: Conducted career-guidance sessions, including one at MV Foundation, inspiring young girls with empowerment stories and the transformative power of education.
  • Health & Hygiene: Through initiatives like the Swasthya Siksha workshop at Sishuvihar orphanage, we spread awareness about hygiene and wellness, supported by pediatric specialists.
  • Leadership & Fellowship: Our members benefit from leadership workshops, board training, and monthly fellowship activities. The 2025–26 roadmap includes adopting schools, launching a global grant, and completing 28 service projects.

    • What Makes Us Unique

  • Youthful Spirit, Big Dreams: Established in 2021, Abhyuday brings fresh energy to classic Rotary service.
  • Holistic Approach: From eco-drives to education and wellness, we balance service with sustainability.
  • Community-Driven: Active Rotarians, Rotaractors, and partners collaborate for real-world impact.
  • Hands-On Growth: Vocational visits, such as to HIMSW Ltd, add value through industry learning and leadership exposure.

    • Rotary Club of Abhyuday Hyderabad invites individuals who are passionate about service, leadership, and fellowship. Together, we strive to build a healthier, stronger, and more inclusive tomorrow—one initiative at a time.
